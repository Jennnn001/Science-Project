HYDROCARBONS

Hydrocarbons are organic compounds made up of carbon and hydrogen. These are organic compounds found in crude oil, gasoline, and industrial waste. In the rainforest, hydrocarbons come from oil spills, vehicle emissions, and factory discharge, polluting water and poisoning wildlife.
